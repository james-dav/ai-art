Art created by Artificial Intelligence

Art and Computers
Computers have been involved in the creation of art for decades but in recent years there has been a huge development of their role in the creative process. The dawn of artificial intelligence and machine learning holds the key to untold possibilities due to its seemingly limitless number of applications. One such application, that yields stunning results, is the creation of images.
Deep Dream
DeepDream is a tool created by Google engineer Alexander Mordvintsev initially to help researchers understand how neural networks perceive images. The program was trained to detect patterns such as faces or animals and enhance them. The psychedelic images created by DeepDream exploded in popularity on the internet in mid-2015.
GANs
Generative Adversarial Networks came along around a similar time and offered incredible results. These neural networks are trained on a dataset of images of a particular subject (e.g. human faces) and can then generate brand new images of this subject. A popular example is thispersondoesnotexist.com, a site created by researchers which allows users to generate new, realistic human faces.
